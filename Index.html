FAQ
How do I request a commission?+
DM me on Twitter/X or Bluesky with your references, a description of what you want, and your chosen options from this sheet. I'll confirm availability and send payment details.
Will my commission be posted on social media?+
If I find it interesting, I may post it on my social media. If you'd prefer it to remain private, just let me know when booking your slot or at any point during the process and I'll respect your wishes.
Can I use the art commercially or post it publicly?+
Personal use and posting with credit is always fine. Commercial use requires prior agreement and may incur additional fees. Please ask before using the art commercially.
What payment methods do you accept?+
I accept PayPal and Ko-fi. Payment is required upfront before I start working. A $5 service fee is applied to cover platform processing costs.
How long will my commission take?+
Delivery is typically between 5 and 15 days depending on complexity and my current queue. I'll give you an estimated timeframe when confirming your slot.
